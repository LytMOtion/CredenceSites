Questions

The details.

Is this a template?+

You choose a refined Credence course system, which we tailor with your identity, photography, content, services, and booking provider.

Can you connect our current tee-time provider?+

In most cases, yes. Your existing reservation platform can remain in place while Credence redesigns the public-facing course website.

What does Website Care & Management include?+

Hosting, SSL, maintenance, backups, monitoring, booking-link checks, routine text updates, and up to three image replacements each month.

What happens if we cancel?+

You may cancel at any time. Your domain and original content remain yours; the hosted Credence site ends with the paid service period unless another arrangement is made.
