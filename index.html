Questions

The details.

Is this a template?+

No. You choose a refined course system, then we tailor it with your identity, content, and booking provider.

Can you connect our current tee-time provider?+

In most cases, yes. Your existing reservation platform stays in place while Credence redesigns the public-facing site.

What does Website Care & Management include?+

The full $250/month scope shown under Pricing — hosting, maintenance, monitoring, booking-link checks, routine text updates, and up to three image replacements a month.

What happens if we cancel?+

You may cancel at any time. Your domain and original content remain yours; the hosted Credence site ends with the paid service period unless another arrangement is made.
