The Problem

A full tee sheet doesn't make the website unimportant.

Plenty of good courses are represented online by a site that's dated, hard to use on a phone, or unclear about rates — quietly costing rounds, events, and first impressions.

Your website is the first round most people play at your course. It's read by destination golfers, tournament organizers, event and wedding planners, diners, lesson and fitting clients, prospective members, and travel writers — long before anyone reaches the first tee.

The System

A proven system, tailored to your property.

Credence doesn't start from an empty page for every course. We work from refined course website systems — design frameworks built specifically for golf — and tailor each one to your identity, photography, content, rates, and booking.

The result is faster delivery, lower setup cost, and consistent technical quality, with a site that reads unmistakably as your course. Mobile-first, with your booking provider connected and the technical side handled for you.

Questions

The details.

Is this a template?+

You're choosing a refined course website system — a proven framework built for golf — that we tailor to your property with your identity, photography, content, and booking. It isn't a generic marketplace template, and your finished site won't be sold to anyone else.

Can you connect our current tee-time provider?+

In most cases, yes — we integrate your existing booking link or tee sheet so reservations continue to run through your provider. Exact compatibility depends on the provider and the access available to us.

Do we need new photography?+

Not to begin. We can build with the imagery you have and clearly-labeled placeholders, and course photography, drone, and video are available separately when you're ready.

What does the monthly care plan include?+

Hosting and SSL, technical maintenance, backups and monitoring, form and link checks, reasonable text edits to existing sections, and up to three image replacements per calendar month. Unused updates don't roll over.

Who owns our domain and content?+

You do. Your domain, logo, photography, and written content remain yours. Credence retains its templates, code, components, and reusable design systems.

What happens if we cancel?+

You can cancel at any time. The hosted site is taken offline at the end of the paid period; your own domain and content remain yours, and source code isn't transferred unless separately agreed.

Do you guarantee accessibility or search rankings?+

No. We implement accessibility-conscious, search-friendly foundations, but we don't guarantee WCAG/ADA compliance, search ranking, traffic, or booking volume.
