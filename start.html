01  Who It's For

Built for the way courses operate.

Credence is a specialized studio for golf-course websites. If you run or market one of these, the system is built for you.

Public courses Daily-fee courses Independent clubs Destination courses Resort golf properties Municipal courses Courses in renovation or repositioning Courses under new ownership

Typically requested by a general manager, owner, director of golf, head professional, or marketing director with authority to approve the project.

02  What To Prepare

Most of it, you already have.

Nothing needs to be perfect to start. Gather what you can, and we'll guide the rest during intake.

i.

Course basics

Official name, location, type, holes, par, and a short sense of what makes the course distinct.

ii.

Golf operations

Your tee-time provider and booking link, rates and rate categories, and key policies.

iii.

Beyond the round

Dining, events, outings, lessons, fitting, membership, and the golf shop, where they apply.

iv.

Brand & imagery

Logo and brand assets, plus any photography or video you already own or are licensed to use.

v.

Access & approvals

Domain registrar access and who signs off — ownership, municipality, management company, or legal.

vi.

Your goals

What's wrong with the current site, and what a better one should do for the course.

03  The Terms, Plainly

No surprises.

i.

$1,000 setup, $150 per month

A one-time setup fee to tailor and launch your site, and a monthly care plan for hosting, maintenance, and defined updates. No long-term contract.

ii.

What the monthly plan covers

Hosting and SSL, technical maintenance, backups and monitoring, form and link checks, reasonable text edits to existing sections, and up to three image replacements per calendar month. Unused updates don't roll over.

iii.

What's outside standard scope

New pages, new functionality, redesigns, extensive content work, and photography, drone, or video production are quoted separately.

iv.

Review & approval

We build from your intake and send a private review link. The site is published only after your written final approval and any ownership, municipal, management, or legal sign-off you require.

v.

After launch

We connect your domain, publish the site, and begin ongoing care. Your care plan runs while the site is live and hosted by Credence.

vi.

If you cancel

Cancel at any time. The hosted site is taken offline at the end of the paid period. Your domain, logo, photography, and written content remain yours; source code isn't transferred unless separately agreed.

vii.

What stays yours

You keep ownership of your domain, logo, photography, and written content.

viii.

What stays ours

Credence keeps its templates, code, components, and reusable design systems.

Full details are in the Website Subscription & Services Agreement.
