Privacy Policy

Effective 2026 · Credence · hello@credencesites.com

This Privacy Policy explains what information Credence collects through this website and how we use it.

1. Information we collect

When you submit our course intake or contact forms, we collect the details you choose to provide — such as your name, job title, business contact information, the course or property name, ownership or management details, and the course information, links, and content you share so we can build and service your website.

2. How we use it

We use this information only to build, deliver, host, and service your website, and to communicate with you about your project. We do not sell your information.

3. How it is handled

Form submissions are transmitted over an encrypted connection and delivered to Credence by email through our third-party form processor (Web3Forms). We retain submissions only as long as needed to provide the service and to maintain reasonable business records.

4. What we do not collect

Credence does not request payment-card numbers, passwords, or confidential access credentials through this website. Please do not submit that information, private employee records, or other sensitive data through our forms. If account access is ever needed to build or maintain your site, we will arrange a secure method separately.

5. Cookies & analytics

This website does not set advertising cookies and does not run third-party analytics or tracking scripts. Web fonts are loaded from Google Fonts, and when your browser requests them, Google may receive standard request information such as your IP address. Our hosting and form providers may log routine technical information (for example, IP address and browser type) as part of delivering and securing the site.

6. Third-party services

We rely on third-party providers for hosting, form processing, email, domain services, and web fonts. These providers process data on our behalf, or receive standard technical request data, in order to deliver the service.

7. Your choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of the information you submitted by contacting us. We will respond to reasonable requests as required by applicable law.

8. Children

Our service is intended for golf-course operators and businesses and is not directed to children.

9. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date above reflects the most recent version.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy may be directed to hello@credencesites.com.
