01 — The Course

Eighteen holes, read like a schedule.

  • Holes18
  • Par72
  • Championship yardage7,240 yds
  • TeesFour options
  • PlayWalk or ride
  • PracticeFull facility

Broad, strategic fairways set up decisions rather than just swings. Sculptural bunkers and firm, fast greens ask you to weigh the aggressive line against the sensible one, hole after hole.

Native desert corridors frame nearly every fairway — the course is generous off the tee, then narrows its demands as you approach each green.
