Groups & Events

Bring the group west.

Corporate golf, private retreats, tournaments, and desert dinners — a full day built around your group, from first light to last call.

Fictional demonstration property. Sample content and reference imagery only.

01 — Arrival

The group arrives, and the desert opens up.

Casitas steps from the first tee, shaded courtyards, and a welcome built for corporate retreats and private groups alike — everyone settled before a single shot is struck.

Coordinated check-in, casita blocks, and a single point of contact from arrival through departure.

02 — First Tee

Every group finds its own start time.

Shotgun starts, staggered tee sheets, or a full tournament field — the format is built around the group, then organized down to the starting assignment.

03 — Retreat

Between rounds, the group slows down.

Private meeting space, golf-specific recovery, and quiet rooms set apart from the course — the middle hours of the day, held for the group alone.

04 — Dinner

The table is set for the whole group.

Private dinners on the terrace, awards under open sky, and milestone celebrations shaped by the desert evening.

05 — Morning

A second round, or a quiet way home.

Some groups take the course again at first light. Others take a slower breakfast and a last look before departure — the same coordination, either way.
